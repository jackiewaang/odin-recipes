Fish and Chips

Description

Simple fish and chips made with cod fillets and twice-fried fries. Really good

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into strips
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 Â½ pounds cod fillets

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes and place in a medium bowl covered with cold water.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper together in a separate medium bowl.
    Add milk and egg; stir until batter is smooth and stand for 20 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in large pot to 175 degrees C.
  4. Drain and pat dry potatoes. FrY in hot oil until tender for 5 minutes; drain on paper towels.
  5. Dredge cod in batter and place in hot oil. Fry fish until golden brown and drain on paper towels.
  6. Fry potatoes again in hot oil until crips for 1/2 minutes; drain on paper towels and serve.
