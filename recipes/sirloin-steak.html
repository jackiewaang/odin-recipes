Sirloin Steak

Description

This recipe will help you cook a sirloin steak just like a fine restaurant

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 pounds beef top sirloin steaks
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium-low heat.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and garlic powder. Set aside.
  4. Season both sides of each steak with salt and pepper.
  5. Place steaks on preheated grill and cook 4/5 minutes per side.
  6. Transfer steaks to warmed plates; brush the tops with garlic butter and rest for 2/3 minutes before serving.
